About the Springboard Series

The Springboard Series contains a number of high-quality app icons purpose-designed for Apple iPhone and iPad devices. Every icon has been tested on a real device and abides by Apple's design guidelines.

Things to Know

  • This series was not designed for, or tested on, Android, Windows or Mac devices. You are welcome to use them for that, but at your own risk.
  • Your device must be running iOS 14 or iPadOS 14 or above.
