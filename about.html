About Us

Why we exist

In a space and time dominated by e-commerce sites and digital merchandising, what sets us apart?

We exist to provide top-quality, design-based content for every customer. We do this by endorsing brilliant designers, striving for their best quality work, and manually checking and testing every product before it goes live on the store to rid every imperfection we can.

We're not robots

We’re designers and developers, and we built this platform from the ground up for you. If you have suggestions, ideas, or queries, we’re open to contact through our social media accounts, or by emailing support@adrodex.com.

Join Us

If you’re interested in developing and selling your own digital merchandise on Adrodex, we’d love to hear from you! Contact us through support@adrodex.com and we’ll see what you can do. If you have a relevant portfolio of work or a concept of your idea/s, show us! It will help your application.

Our Brand

We take our brand, design and public image seriously, and we find it important to correctly and accurately represent what we do. To assist with this, we’ve developed a marketing package containing all the imagery and textual resources you need, plus a best practices guide to ensure our brand is shown in all the right ways.

We kindly ask you to read through this before using our logo, typeface, or any other likeness in any work or promotion. Once you have done this, we’d love to see what you’ve made! Tag us on social media, or contact us at support@adrodex.com.
